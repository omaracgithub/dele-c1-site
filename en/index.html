Prepare for the DELE C1 on your iPhone

The Diploma de Español como Lengua Extranjera (DELE) C1 certifies advanced proficiency in Spanish. It is issued by the Instituto Cervantes on behalf of Spain's Ministry of Education and does not expire. The DELE C1 is internationally recognized by universities, employers, and official institutions.

The exam consists of 4 tests grouped into 2 scoring groups. Group 1 includes reading comprehension and use of language (90 minutes, 5 tasks) and listening comprehension and use of language (50 minutes, 5 tasks). Group 2 includes integrated skills: listening comprehension and written expression (80 minutes, 2 tasks) and reading comprehension and oral expression (20 minutes preparation + 20 minutes, 3 tasks).

To pass, you need a minimum of 30 out of 50 points in each group. The maximum score is 100. DELE C1 Prep lets you practice all tests with exercises that follow the official format, so you arrive at the exam with confidence.
