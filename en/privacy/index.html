Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Information we collect

DELE C1 Prep collects the minimum information necessary for the app to function:

  • Anonymous usage data: Aggregated statistics about app usage (screens visited, exercises completed) through Firebase Analytics. This data does not personally identify you.
  • Subscription data: Managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.
  • User progress: Your progress is stored locally on your device and optionally synced through your Apple account (iCloud).

2. Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect payment information
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties for advertising

3. Third-party services

We use the following services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google): For anonymous usage statistics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing. Apple Privacy Policy

4. Data storage

Your progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. If you use iCloud, this data may sync across your Apple devices. We do not maintain our own servers with user data.

5. Data deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from the app: Account → Delete Account. You can also uninstall the app, which will remove all local data.

6. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will post any changes on this page with the updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@prepdele.com.
